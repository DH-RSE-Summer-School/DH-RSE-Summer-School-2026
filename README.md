# Digital Humanities & Research Software Engineering Summer School 2026
## 29 June - 2 July 2026

## About

Welcome to the repository of the [DH & RSE Summer School 2026](https://dh-rse-summer-school.github.io/DH-RSE-Website/2026.html). Here you will 
find all materials relating to the Summer School held at University of Manchester.

The Summer School is a partnership between [Software Sustainability Institute](https://www.software.ac.uk/), 
[Cambridge Digital Humanities](https://www.cdh.cam.ac.uk/) (University of Cambridge), 
[King's Digital Lab](https://kdl.kcl.ac.uk/) (King's College London), 
and [Centre for Data, Culture & Society](https://www.cdcs.ed.ac.uk/) 
(University of Edinburgh), in collaboration with [Digital Skills in Arts and Humanities](https://www.diskah.org/) (DISKAH) project. 
This year the Software Sustainability Institute is hosting the event at the [University of Manchester](https://www.manchester.ac.uk/). 

**Partner institution leads**: [Oscar Seip](https://www.software.ac.uk/our-people/oscar-seip), 
[Neil Jakeman](https://kdl.kcl.ac.uk/who-we-are/neil-jakeman/), 
[Mary Chester-Kadwell](https://www.cdh.cam.ac.uk/about/people/dr-mary-chester-kadwell/), 
[Jonathan Blaney](https://www.cdh.cam.ac.uk/about/people/jonathan-blaney/),
[Lucia Michelin](https://www.cdcs.ed.ac.uk/about)

Work in progress: This repository is under construction and details will continue to be added during the event.
